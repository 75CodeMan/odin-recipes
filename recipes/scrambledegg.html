Scrambled Eggs

Perfect side for breakfast

  • 4 Eggs
  • 1 tbps Butter
  1. Preheat the skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Crack your eggs into a bowl and whisk together
  3. Put your butter in the skillet and allow to melt then incorperate your whisked eggs
  4. Stir eggs in the until cooked to your liking
  5. Remove from the skillet and enjoy!!!
