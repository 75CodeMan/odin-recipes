Amazing TailGate Dip

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion (diced)
  • 3 Jalapenos (diced)
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 2 lbs ground meat
  • 2 Tbps worcestershire sauce
  • 16 oz Medium Cheddar
  • 16 oz Pepper Jack
  • 2 Tbps Cajun Seasoning
  • 12 oz Evaporated milk
  • 8 oz Cream Cheese
  • 12 oz Cooked and Minced Bacon
  • 2 Tbps Minced Garlic

Instructions

  1. Cook your 2 lbs of ground meat in a skillet

  2. Combine all your ingredients into a crockpot

  3. Cook on low for 2 hours(stirring every 30 minutes)

  4. Enjoy your tailgate party and dig in
